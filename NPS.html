What is NPS?

The National Pension System (NPS) is a government-backed retirement savings scheme designed to help individuals build a secure financial future. By investing systematically during your working years, NPS creates a corpus for post-retirement income, ensuring stability and peace of mind.

It blends the best of equity (growth potential) and debt (stability), offering a market-linked yet risk-balanced approach to retirement planning.

Key Benefits of NPS

Disciplined Wealth Creation

Encourages long-term savings for retirement with a structured approach.

Flexibility

Choose asset allocation between equities, corporate debt, and government securities.

Professional Fund Management

Managed by top pension fund managers regulated by PFRDA.

Tax Efficiency

Enjoy additional tax benefits under Section 80CCD(1B), beyond Section 80C.

Dual Benefit

Build a retirement corpus and secure lifelong pension via annuity.

Low Cost Structure

One of the lowest-cost retirement products available in India.

Who Should Invest in NPS?

  • Young Professionals: Start early and let compounding work for decades.
  • Mid-Career Individuals: Balance risk and stability while accelerating corpus growth.
  • Business Owners / Self-Employed: Access a government-backed retirement structure without employer dependency.
  • Late Starters: Even starting closer to retirement helps secure a pension cushion.
